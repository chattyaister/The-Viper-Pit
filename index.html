The Viper Pit

The Viper Pit has existed for 10 years, and an additional 20 more years under different leaders and under the name of the Vermillion Pact. The organization was overthrown and taken over by Wisteria and Aluran 10 or so years ago, and expanded rapidly from there. It is a rapidly growing syndicate situated in Mistral.

The Viper Pit deals primarily in two major resources- Drugs, in the form of "Hush" and "Venom", created after a long period of questionable experimenatiaion by the founder Aluran, and information, primarly on Huntsmen, Huntresses, Students, Villains and Villainesses,

Despite their relatively positive front, the orgnaization partakes in many morally gray and illegal activities in their expansive underground network.

Vermillion Pact

Before the Viper Pit, was the Vermillion Pact. The Vermillion Pact was a crime institution run by a select group of figures that named themselves the Vermillion Hand. Operating under the guise of binding pacts, contracts, and agreements, they loosely justified the atrocities committed through the means of trades and obligations.

The Pact preyed on the addicted, the needy and the desperate- those who were willing to sign their futures away for immediate profit or relief. In their systems, the signed pact was law. Those who could or would not complete its terms resulted in the signeeРђЎs forcible collection from their homes, from whence they would be funneled into the black market- in parts, or as whole. Within years, the Pact grew within Mistral, becoming a structure of exploitation, a system of debt and ownership of people.

The downfall begins with a young woman from outside, and a young man from inside.

The Disappearances

When everyday, low-tier members within the Pact went missing, it was deemed insignificant. Disappearances within Mistral were far from uncommon, and the disappearances were isolated enough to not warrant immediate follow-up, simply deemed statistical noise.

The statistical noise began to repeat.

A courier, dead two districts from his designated path. A collector, dead face down in a pond outside his home. An enforcer, a contract handler, their family members. Each body was found with a single stalk of wisteria tucked into their hands. When the incidents began to compound, whispers began first in the lower ranking members of the Pact - of how a higher ranking member, someone in the Hand, had authorized the pruning of their own rankings and structures. Rumors that spread well, given the alternative would be that there was someone outside, that had learned their way into their systems.

When the next bodies were found, it was clear. The attacks were no longer random. It had become precise, calculated, targeted. The lilac haired boy never interfered directly in the affairs of the Pact. He never fought, never lied, rather, he rerouted, altered truths, omitted facts. He specialized in truth- he simply decided who deserved the entirety of it. Information never quite made it fully intact, and what information made it to the correct people were half-truths and missing details, with the rest always arriving a hint too late. When information became untrustworthy, things began to rapidly spiral.

It was at this point, the Hand would attempt to correct itself.

Internal audits intensified, communications became restricted, members were reassigned into tighter clusters in an attempt to slow the spiral- yet the deaths continued. Not faster, not flashier, simply continued, at their usual pace, like she knew what she was doing.

He moved the pieces, and she hunted.
