Combat Data

Range: Close-Ranged Combat

Notable Traits: Her attacks are both heavy and swift,

Threat Level: Unclear

Asphodel and a glock

An extension of her self, she wields the scythe with comfort and ease.

Asphodel is an ornately designed scythe, a little taller than Ai herself, adorned in crystals and feathers. The weapon does not change forms at all, nor minimize in size aside from the blade folding in towards the handle- the Reaper carries the weapon in it's full size everywhere she goes, nestled carefully between her wings.

Despite it's large size, the scythe is fairly lightweight due to the blade being infused with gravity dust, and the gravity dust crystals embedded into the weapon at various points throughout. Using the gravity dust, the Reaper swings the weapon without much effort, activating the dust at various points to add weight to her attacks or change the trajectory of her weapon. She has been known to throw the entire freaking weapon at her enemy, miss, only to recall the weapon with the dust back to her into the back of her cackling enemy's head.

She wears a pair of sleeves, which the inner lining is woven with gravity dust, that allows her to recall Asphodel to her.

She has a glock. A plain, non-transformative, no dust infused, glock. holds 17+1 rounds.
