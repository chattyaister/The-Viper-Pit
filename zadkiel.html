Psychological Assessment

Quite pleasant, he is a soft spoken and gentle soul who loves to help out where he can. He helps anyone he can help without question, without considering whether the person he’s assisting is good or bad. It makes him quite easy to manipulate.

He is generally calm, and likes to partake in quiet activities like listening to soft music, sitting out in nature, talking to and feeding wildlife, etc. he also likes to hang in places like parks, listening to people go by as he enjoys a sweet drink or two.

Though he enjoys the company of others, he does not like crowded, noisy places like bars or cafeterias, as they get very quickly overwhelming for his sensitive ears. When overwhelmed, he hides away alone to try and calm his senses.

He is blissfully unaware, seeing the best in people whether they deserve it or not- he has the tendency to misinterpret intent to fit his endlessly positive world view. He is naive and confused, unable to see the evil that surrounds him.

Likes

Dislikes

Feeling helpless. He was helpless when he lost what mattered most to him. He won't let it happen again.

Good Habits

A kind soul to the core, he will help out wherever and whenever and however he can. He is quick to like people, and quick to forgive, and often checks on the people around him to make sure everyone is doing alright.

He will often offer a helping hand to those in need without being asked, simply offering what he can give because... well, he can.

Bad Habits

He does not ask questions, and accept things for how they are. This makes him overlook obvious red flags, and remain blissfully unaware of the chaos that surrounds him.

He enables chaos, whether he means to or not, by simply existing within proximity of the wrong population

He is trusting to a fault.

Secrets

His semblance first activated with the death of his brother. It could not, in fact, bring his dead brother back. He mourns that fact with his enitre being.

Fears

Death. He remembers the night he lost his brother.

Becoming useless. He does not know it himself, but he has a fear of losing his use, as that makes him easier to abandon.

Recovered Origin Records

Not Quite Home

His memory of his mother is hazy at best. His father was an abuser, whose anger bled onto the entire family. He doesn’t remember much of his childhood, aside from hiding in the corners of his home away from his father when he flew into his rage fueled rampages through the house. Furniture would break, items thrown, bottles broken and fresh wounds opened every time his father would enter one of his rampages. He huddled, his hands over his ears and his eyes shut tight, counting numbers until the screams would finally give way to sobs and eventual silence. He never looked his mother in the eyes, and she did not care.

Initially these rampages were directed at their mother. Aside from catching the occasional crossfire, the boy went mostly unharmed. When the boy turned five, the anger would turn to the young boy, finding the small malnourished child an easier target than someone of his own stature. A perfect target for a coward.

Bruises.

Scratches.

Cuts.

Abrasions.

Burns.

Wounds small and big littered his small frame, yet no one ever knew, as no one ever saw. The boy did not step outside, he was not allowed to, and he did not question it, as that is how his life had been. His world did not extend beyond his abusers.

Little Light

His brother was born when he was 7. He was a tiny little thing, a small anchor in the place of chaos, a shred of light in the dark of the Sheep’s life. When his tiny little hand held his thin, frail finger, he felt warmth he had not felt in the years he had existed within this lonesome world. Just like that, his little brother had become the center of his empty world.

He protected his brother with all his little being could muster.

He’d starve so that his brother could have a slightly fuller meal, he’d always make sure his father’s attention never turned when he was enraged, he’d sit with the child, hurt and broken, so that the child did not know what it meant to be lonely. His little brother grew knowing violence never a target of it, knowing hunger but never starving, unlike his older brother, the boy grew knowing what warmth felt like, what gentle hands brought, and what laughter and giggles sounded like. He made sure of it.

He’d do anything for his precious little brother, the light of his world.

A Tragedy

His life wasn’t always tragedy, he had his little brother. Had.

It was when the Sheep was 12 years of age, and his little brother the young age of 5, when his father had gone into another one of his rampages, that his true tragedy began, when the world that gave him one little light decided to smother it out once again.

He did not wince much as kicks met flesh, he simply curled along the floor as he always did, wrapping his head in his arms. He did not plead, nor cry, nor scream, simply beared as the drunken man, a bottle of whisky in hand, kicked at the helpless boy who so much as made a sound. His younger brother watched from the corner of the room, huddled in fear like the sheep always had done for countless nights alone. But the Sheep was no longer alone. So he took the blows without a word.

A Dagger?

A plain, good old trusty dagger. It does not change forms, however, it does contain a little compartment that allows for dust use. He uses lightning dust generally, for a taser-like effect to his attacks that buys him sufficiant time to escape the confrontation.

In his dagger as well as a few extra vials to refill his dagger with.

Strenghts

Keen Senses. Because he is blind, his other senses have become keen to compensate. His sense of smell and hearing are above average to even that of other Faunus, making stealthily around Kiel immensely difficult, as he picks up the faintest of shuffling about.

Familiarity. He rarely strays from familiar places, and knows the locations he frequents like the palm of his hand. He uses his familiarity to his advantage, making use of everything in his surrounding that he can to escape a fight.

Weaknesses

Pacifistic Nature A bit of a pacifist, he prefers not to fight if possible and will avoid confrontation like the plague. When engaged in a fight, he is more concerned about escape than actually winning said fight.

Blindness His blindness plays a critical role in disabling his ability to fight. Though able to sense his enemies in other ways, he is clumsy and disheveled in a fight, especially if he is in an unfamiliar setting.

Semblance: Healing Presence

The ram’s semblance is a for the most part passive type, although it is not entirely uncontrollable. His semblance has the effect of healing everyone within a certain proximity of himself, mending any physical wounds obtained in battle or accident. And while his semblance cannot be ‘shut off’ at will, it’s area of effect can be reduced for an increase in intensity.

At the maximum area of effect of 15 ft radius centered around him, his healing effects are minor but noticeable. At maximum range, minor injuries such as cuts and bruises still heal at an accelerated rate, a fresh wound fading within seconds of his presence. More grave injuries will heal slowly, and while at maximum range his semblance is only strong enough to prolong someone’s life until proper help arrives.

If needed, however, the radius of his healing can be reduced in exchange for an increased healing potential. At the lowest range, his healing is limited to those who are physically in contact with the faunus, and with the intensity a healthy individual has the potential of surviving a fatal injury when in contact with him, recovering from their critical state in approximately thirty or so minutes. He can save a person from the grips of death so long as he is able to remain in contact with them long enough.

A very faint sweet scent hangs in the air where his semblance is active, a scent that many find calming. The scent carries a minor buffing effect, subduing the effect of pain in those affected- injuries simply hurt less when within his magic field, allowing those affected to continue fighting without being incapacitated by wounds. It has the unintended effect of allowing fights to go on longer than they should.

Although the semblance does require aura, the amount of aura it uses over time simply falls under his recovery of aura over time. Because of this, while his semblance does not diminish his aura, breaking his aura shield naturally shuts off his semblance. Despite the fact that his semblance does not drain his aura, keeping its area of effect reduced to heal a single target for an extended amount of time seems to tire him out significantly.

Although the semblance does not shut down until his aura is broken, once his aura has been broken his semblance does not return until his aura has completely replenished… and so breaking his aura once, and then constantly keeping his aura below the 100% threshold is an effective method of negating his semblance. Once his aura shield is broken, it takes three hours of undisturbed rest to recover fully.

While his semblance remains active while he is asleep, being forcefully knocked unconscious, or losing consciousness due to overexertion, will shut it off.

His semblance affects only those who do not actively reject it. While his semblance heals everyone within his proximity that is willing, or is simply unaware, those who are aware of his semblance and reject it does not receive its healing effects.

The semblance does not distinguish friend or foe, and will heal anyone regardless of their alignment and their relationship to him, as long as they remain within its area of effect.

His semblance does not affect himself.

His semblance does not replenish other people’s aura.

Activation Cue: The radius of his healing can be determined by a translucent, purple field that is ever present around him, as well as the sweet flowery scent that lingers the air about him.
